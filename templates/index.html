Spotify Tools

A personal toolkit for building and managing Spotify playlists.

{% if not logged_in %} Connect with Spotify {% endif %}

🎵 Block Mix

Select 2 or more playlists and build a new playlist where songs rotate in blocks — 4 from one genre, then 4 from another, like the old Pandora experience. Supports repeats and full randomization.

Open Block Mix

💥 Album Blaster

Browse any playlist, pick 5–30 tracks, and blast their full albums into a brand new playlist. Great for rediscovering albums you forgot you loved.

Open Album Blaster

🗂️ Manage Playlists

Browse all your playlists in a filterable, sortable table. Preview tracks, toggle public/private visibility, and bulk delete playlists you no longer need.

Open Manager

🏷️ Playlist Tags

Tag your playlists with free-form labels like "chill", "office", or "instrumental". Tags carry into Block Mix as a filter so you can quickly build the right vibe without hunting through 500+ playlists.

Manage Tags
